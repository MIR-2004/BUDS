Squair Pod
Squair Pod is a new Bluetooth earphone design concept inspired by a round square shape. Squair Pod's parts consist of three parts cover, middle, and bottom. It is designed to maintain the previous part separation line, making it easy to assemble and disassemble, and finishing the parts that are seen when worn in a organized shape. Squair Pod's charger has a built-in LED lighting on the front that displays the state of charge and Bluetooth connection. The entire top is made up of a thin, high-light sliding magnet cover. The focus was on designing all products to share the same design language by applying the concept of the same shape to Squair Pod chargers.
